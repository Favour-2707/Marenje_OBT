The Marenje Team

Dedicated to bringing God's Word to the Marenje people

The Marenje OBT is part of The Word For The World, working tirelessly to translate the Bible from English into Marenje language. Our mission is to ensure that everyone can hear and read the Word of God in their heart language.

Our Goals

  • Ensure everyone has access to the Word of God
  • Translate the entire Bible by 2050
  • Connect people beyond language barriers
